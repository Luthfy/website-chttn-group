ABOUT RUPAKA STORE

SHOES ARE MORE THAN JUST A STYLE.
IT’S A MOVEMENT. IT’S A PRIDE.
IT’S INDONESIAN-MADE ORIGINAL.

Rupaka store firstly opened in 2017. The concept of our store is rather simple and intentional.
We want to be a place of the best, high-quality Indonesian-made shoes where people can come and buy their favorite local brands. For the past couple of years, wearing local-made shoes is more than just wearing shoes, it is a movement and the expression of pride and
love to the local craft. Therefore, we want to be a part of this community where wearing
a local-made is awesome.

WHERE TO FIND US.

Located in Cempaka street, Banjarmasin, Rupaka store is one door away from Chat Barber and Chatbox coffee shop. We are open from 10am until 10pm everyday. Come and visit our store, click link down below for Google map direction.
