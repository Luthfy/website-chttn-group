ABOUT CHAT BARBER

CHAT BARBER IS WHERE THE GENTLEMAN
GETS HIS HAIRCUT.

Chat Barber opened in 2017, being one of the barber shop pioneer in Banjarmasin. Chat
Barber provides variety of services like cut and wash, cut only, shaving, or wash only.
Our shop is a client-driven friendly atmosphere in which we want every client feels
welcomed and comfortable. We highly value our relationship with our customers. We
serve all. Our team is highly skilled to provide the best haircuts for men or even your
little boys. We recruit the best talent and provide them with the education and resources
so that client will get the best of haircut that you love and proud of.
