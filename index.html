HOW IT STARTED

STARTING THE FIRST BUSINESS IS ALWAYS A BIG CHALLENGE. STARTING ANOTHER ONE IS OFTEN A PLEASURE.

RIZKI MAULIDIN, CHATATAN GROUP FOUNDER

Our story began in 2015, started with Chat Barber as one of the barbershop pioneers in Banjarmasin. Chat Barber is known for its friendly barber man whom our customers love to talk to. So, it is definitely more than getting a good haircut and good relaxation, but also it’s how our customers love being listened to every story and trust that they share with us. The relationship between us and the customers is one thing that we care about and are proud of the most. Now, Chat Barber has expanded into 3 branches thanks to the support from our customers.

After successfully starting Chat Barber, we began to expand our business branch into a shoe cleaning service in 2016. We named it Chat Shoe Treatment. It’s like the brother of Chat Barber but with different expertise. At the time, our peers might wonder why we took that road. The simplest answer is how our founder loves sneaker collecting. So, they know exactly how to give special care and good treatment to shoes, so that they can last long. Again, this is an opportunity for us to give a good service to people who want to take care of their favorite shoes in professional hands by opening this service. So far, Chat Shoe Treatment has 3 other branches that are located in Banjarmasin and Banjarbaru.

Today, Chatatan Group has established 5 brands in total including Rupaka [Shoe Store], Chat Box [Coffee Shop], and CHTTN [Clothing Brand]. Also, adding another business portfolio with 2 property businesses in house renting and guest house.
