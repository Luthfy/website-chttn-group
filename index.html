ABOUT US
HOW IT STARTED

"STARTING THE FIRST BUSINESS IS ALWAYS A BIG CHALLENGE. STARTING ANOTHER ONE IS OFTEN A PLEASURE."

RIZKI MAULIDIN, CHATATAN GROUP FOUNDER

Our story begins in 2015, started with Chat Barber as one of the barbershop pioneer in Banjarmasin. Chat Barber is known for its friendly barberman whom our customers love to talk to. So, it is definetely more than getting a good haircut and good relaxation, but also it’s how our customers love being listened to every story and trust that they share with us. The relationship between us and the customers is one thing that we care and are proud of the most. Now, Chat Barber has expand into 3 branches thanks to the support and from our customers.

After, successfully started Chat Barber, we began to expand our business branch into a shoe cleaning service in 2016. We named it Chat Shoe Treatment. It’s like the brother of Chat Barber, but in different expertise. At the time, our peers might wonder why we took that road? The simplest answer is how our founder love shoes collecting. So, they know exactly how to give a special care and good treatment for shoes, so that it can last long. Again, this is an opportunity for us to give a good service to people who wants to take care of their favorite shoes in professional hands by opening this service. So far, Chat Shoe Treatment have 3 other branches that are located in Banjarmasin and Banjarbaru.

Until today, we now have 5 brands in total including Rupaka [Shoe Store], Chat Box [Coffee Shop], and CHTTN [Clothing Brand]. Also, adding another business portofolio with 2 property business in a house renting and a guest house.
