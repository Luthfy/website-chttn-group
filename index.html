About Chatbox Coffee

COFFEE FOR EVERYONE TO LOVE.
SIMPLE MADE, NOT COMPLICATED.

Opened in 2021, here in Chatbox, we love making coffee that you can enjoy in every sip. Chatbox coffee is a perfect company whether you are busy working or just chilling in your free time. Our coffee shop is compact and small, making it easy for you who just want to grab and go whenever you're in a rush. Our friendly barista would love to quickly make the coffee for you. Also, our customers often times love to watch our barista taking action when they make latte art because for sure it is enjoyable to watch. Our menu is simple so that you won’t confuse about what to order.

WHERE TO FIND US.

Located in Cempaka street, Banjarmasin, our coffee shop is exactly side by side with Chat Barber, a gentleman haircut barbershop. We operate daily, morning until evening, 10am - 11pm. Come visit and let our barista serve and make you some espresso.
